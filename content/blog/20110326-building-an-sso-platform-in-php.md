# Building an SSO platform in PHP

> For public applications OAuth and OpenID are useful solutions, both of which are presented with code samples in the presentation. For enterprise applications, SAML is a useful standard. The presentation covers building an Identity Provider for your company and shows how to integrate with that identity provider from your applications.
